About Us

Edutainment that moves communities from awareness to action.

Headquartered in the heart of Kibera informal settlement, Wasanii Sanaa blends entertainment with education to address poverty, gender inequality, and human rights violations. Theatre is powerful because it speaks to the heart and the mind — making the invisible visible, giving voice to the unheard.
