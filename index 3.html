About Us

Edutainment that moves communities from awareness to action.

Headquartered in the heart of Kibera informal settlement, Wasanii Sanaa blends entertainment with education to address poverty, gender inequality, and human rights violations. Theatre is powerful because it speaks to the heart and the mind — making the invisible visible, giving voice to the unheard.

Achievements

A decade of partnership and progress

🌍 Performed at the 2025 XVIII Festival Sztuki Ludowej (Poland) and Sanio Mondial Festivals (Italy).
🤝 Peaceful coexistence campaigns with Life & Peace Institute and Mercy Corps Kenya.
🤝 GBV reduction with Gender Violence Recovery Centre and Women Empowerment Link.
🤝 Anti-corruption forum theatre with Transparency International Kenya.
🤝 Human-rights education in schools with Amnesty International Kenya.
🤝 HIV/AIDS stigma reduction with the National AIDS Control Council.
🏆 International Nairobi Trade Fair every year, 2014–2025.
🥇 2018 Gender Thone Winners — CREAW.
